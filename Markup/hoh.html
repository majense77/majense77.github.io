University of Nebraska
2006

Noticing the Nebraska Cornhusker Football team's inefficient use of DVDs to watch and exchange game video, three students in the Jeffrey S. Raikes School decided to create a better solution for the team. In 2006, John Wirtz, Brian Kaiser, and David Graff created

HUDDLE

-an online video platform for the Huskers' game film.

Moving Up, Moving Out

Starting off in a small office on 9th Street in Lincoln, the company made the jump to a new office nicknamed the "Candy Factory" in 2008. Located in the historic Haymarket disrict, this office would house the entire company until mid-2010.

It wouldn't be until then that one of the main offices, now known as "Hudl West" would be opened. The building then housed the entire company - the product team, support, sales, and the business team - all under one roof.
