Interface Inventory

Much like a physical inventory of products, an interface inventory takes stock and categorizes the components that comprise a software solution.
Taking these individual, disparate pieces in bulk lets us to keep a pulse on the state of our products' interfaces, and allows us to chart progress in implementing a design system.

An interface inventory is a comprehensive collection
of the bits and pieces that make up your interface.

- Brad Frost
